How To Play

  • Use the Magma Extractor to collect Magma Balls from the screen! You can collect more than one Magma Ball at a time by grabbing balls while already holding magma.
  • Release the Magma Balls you've collected. Form columns of three or more to cause a chain reaction and destroy all adjacent Magma Balls!
  • Try to clear the screen before you are crushed by the Magma Balls!
  • Volatile Magma Balls are marked with special symbols. If you destroy one of these, all balls of the same color will disappear from the screen!
  • Set up combos for bonus points!
